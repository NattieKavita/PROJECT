Who are We?

FBS believes that everyone deserves access to nutritious food. We are a Kenyan-based non-profit organization committed to providing meals and food assistance to individuals and families experiencing food insecurity.We understand that hunger can have a devastating impact on health, education, and overall well-being. Our programs include: Community outreach programs to help fight food insecurity. We strive to build a community where everyone has the opportunity to thrive. Your support is crucial in helping us achieve this goal. Every donation, no matter the size, makes a significant difference in the lives of those we serve.
