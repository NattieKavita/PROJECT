MISION

To alleviate hunger and food insecurity within our community by creating a sustainable and equitable food distribution network that connects surplus food with those in need, fostering dignity, and promoting self-sufficiency through education, outreach, and community partnerships

VISION

To envision a community where hunger is eradicated, where all individuals and families have access to nutritious and affordable food, and where every member thrives with dignity and self-sufficiency
